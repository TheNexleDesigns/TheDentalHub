Policies

Our Practice Policies.

In place to provide timely service and be respectful to our dentists and all patients.

Late Arrival — 10 & 15 Minute Rules

If you arrive more than 10 minutes late, you are not guaranteed to be seen immediately. Arriving more than 15 minutes late incurs a $50 late fee.

Cancellation Policy — $150 Fee

Cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment. Monday appointments must be changed by Saturday. Cancellations within 24 hours incur a $150 fee. No-shows will not be given another appointment.

Two Consecutive Cancellations

If two consecutive appointments are cancelled, you will not be given another booked appointment. You may only be seen as a walk-in patient going forward.

Chair-Time Fee

If you book a procedure but decide not to proceed on the day, a chair-time fee will be charged to cover the time reserved for your treatment.

Appointment Reminders via WhatsApp

You'll be contacted three times before your appointment — 1 week before, 2 days before, and 24 hours before. No confirmation = automatic cancellation.

New Patients

All new patients must complete a full dental assessment on their first visit before any treatment begins.
